Weekly Devotion: New Song, New Heart

Date: January 10, 2026 | Scripture: Psalm 40:3 | Theme: Refreshing our worship for a new year.

Welcome, Worshippers!

Ready to refresh your worship? Scroll down and let's dive in together.

1. What's New in 2026?

Hey, you! It's 2026—resolutions fading? In Music Ministry, we're chasing a new song, not just a new leaf.

Think: Has your worship turned into a rerun? Same chords, same words, no heart? Let's change that!

2. The Scripture Read

Listen up—let's hear Psalm 40:3

"He has put a new song in my mouth, praise to our God; many will see it and fear, and will trust in the Lord."

Feel that? God's giving you a fresh tune!

3. The Talking Points: The "3S"

The Source (The "He"): Your new song comes from God, not gadgets. Stale worship? Time for more Him!

The Sound (The "New Song"): It's a fresh view of His mercies. Sing that old hymn like it's your story this week.

The Spectacle (The "Many Will See"): People watch your worship first. Make it real—they'll trust God too!

You in? Let's make worship alive!

4. Point to Ponder: Group Discussion

Question: How will you keep worship fresh in 2026—not just routine?

Share with someone: Pray over setlists, dig into lyrics, check your heart before performing.

Speak up—what's one way you'll try this week?

5. The "Grace Baptist" Challenge

This week, find one thing God did for you in the last 7 days. Sing/play from that gratitude—keep the song new!

You ready? Go refresh your worship!

THANK YOU

Thanks for joining, friends! May your worship spark joy and draw others to God in 2026.
